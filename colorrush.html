Game Information

How to Play?
Press START and quickly click on coins appearing on screen. Earn 1-100 ASRA per coin. 10,000 ASRA = 1 TON.
Be Careful!
Red glow behind coin = -100 ASRA. Watch out!
Your Profile
The badge in top right corner shows your Telegram name initials. Your wallet address is also saved after connecting wallet.
Wallet Connection
First connect your wallet using the Wallet button to withdraw TON. Connect Telegram Wallet, Tonkeeper or other wallets via TON Connect. For security, withdrawals are only allowed to your own wallet address.
Withdraw TON
• Collect at least 10 TON
• 1 TON reserved for blockchain fee
• Can withdraw: balance - 1 TON
• Wallet connection required - unauthorized withdrawal impossible
• Blockchain rules: 1 TON fee per transaction
Pay TON
• Pay 1 TON to start real game
• Pay via QR code or from wallet
• Can withdraw TON after payment
