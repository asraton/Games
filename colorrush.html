How to Play?
Press START and quickly click on coins appearing on screen. Earn ASRA based on your selected coin price. Red glow behind coin = -100 ASRA penalty.

HELP - This information page
Your Profile
The badge in top right corner shows your Telegram name initials. Your wallet address is also saved after connecting wallet.
Wallet Connection
First connect your wallet using the Wallet button to withdraw ASRA. Connect Telegram Wallet, Tonkeeper or other wallets via TON Connect. For security, withdrawals are only allowed to your own wallet address.
Withdraw ASRA
• Collect at least 10,000 ASRA
• 10,000 ASRA stays as commission (cannot withdraw)
• Can withdraw: balance - 10,000 ASRA
• Example: 25,000 ASRA → withdraw 15,000 (10,000 stays)
• Wallet connection required
• Need 10,000 ASRA minimum to withdraw again
Pay TON
• Pay 1 TON to start real game
• Pay via QR code or from wallet
• Can withdraw ASRA after payment

⚠️ Important Notice - Monthly Reset
Game Restart Schedule:
On the 1st day of every month, the game performs a complete restart.

What to do on the 1st:
Press the RESTART button in the WALLET panel to refresh the game and receive new updates.

Monthly Bonus:
On the 1st of each month, all users with connected wallets automatically receive 1,000 ASRA as a bonus for the monthly reset inconvenience.
• Bonus is given only once per month per user
• Connect your wallet before the 1st to receive the bonus automatically

Action Required:
Please withdraw all your ASRA to your wallet before the 1st of each month to avoid losing your progress.

Your Wallet is Safe:
This reset applies only to game progress. Your connected wallet and any ASRA already withdrawn to it remain completely safe and unaffected.

Disclaimer:
ASRA Team is not responsible for any ASRA lost due to failure to withdraw before the monthly reset. Please manage your game time accordingly.
